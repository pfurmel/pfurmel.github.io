Individual builds

These are individual builds requested by customers.

Music Thing Turing Machine

This is a Music Thing Modular Turing Machine Eurorack module. The module is inspired by the Electro-Music Klee sequencer, which is an advanced step squencer based on multiple shift registers. I was given the parts for this which were purchased from Thonk and I built and calibrated it.

Panel

The panel used is by Greyscale. They design beautiful after market panels for a lot of the popular modules available.

Thomas Henry 555VCO

This is a 555 VCO Eurorack module. The module was designed by Thomas Henry and the pcb's and panels are from Fonik. I was given the bare pcb's and panels and I built and calibrated them.

The panel used is by Fonik
